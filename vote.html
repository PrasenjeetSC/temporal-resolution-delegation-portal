Resolution A/RES/0425 (V)

Recognizing the necessity of temporal precision in informal communications,

Acknowledging the recurring ambiguity in the use of terms such as "evening" and "night",

Recalling the need for internal standardization across all parties involved,

Determined to minimize interpretational conflict in casual scheduling,


1. Defines “Morning” as 00:00 hrs to 11:59 hrs IST

2. Declares “Afternoon” as 12:00 hrs to 16:59 hrs IST

3. Recognizes “Evening” as 17:00 hrs to 20:59 hrs IST

4. Affirms “Night” as 21:00 hrs to 23:59 hrs IST

5. Establishes this lexicon as binding in all future informal communications

6. Requests delegates to ratify via affirmative vote
